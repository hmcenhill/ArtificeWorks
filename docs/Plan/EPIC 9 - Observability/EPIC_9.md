## [EPIC] Observability

**Labels:** epic, observability, infra
**Milestone:** M5

## Summary

Add structured logging, metrics, and optional traces/dashboarding.

## Why

Observability makes the async workflow understandable and supports debugging and demos.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Correlation IDs are logged
- [ ] Metrics expose throughput/failure/retry counts
- [ ] One order can be traced through multiple stages
